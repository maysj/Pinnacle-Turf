Pinnacle Turf Specialist

Who Are We?

Pinnacle Turf Specialist is a new landscaping company based in Hudson, Ohio, dedicated to providing exceptional lawn care services to the local community and beyond. Owner, Mike Grace, brings a wealth of experience to Pinnacle Turf Specialist. After a successful career spanning many years in the extermination industry, Mike decided to follow his passion for landscaping and leverage his extensive equipment collection to establish a new venture.

A Focus on Quality and Collaboration

Pinnacle Turf Specialist prioritizes providing top-notch lawn care services, ensuring your property boasts a healthy and vibrant green space. Mike's commitment to client satisfaction is paramount, and he strives to build genuine relationships built on trust and open communication.

Expanding Expertise Through Partnership

To provide a well-rounded landscaping experience, Pinnacle Turf Specialist has partnered with a local plant nursery. This collaboration allows them to cater to all your outdoor needs, from lawn care and maintenance to plant selection and installation. Pinnacle Turf Specialist is eager to partner with you to transform your lawn into a source of pride. Whether you require routine maintenance or a complete landscape overhaul, their team is equipped and enthusiastic to bring your vision to life.
